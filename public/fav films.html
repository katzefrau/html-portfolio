Favorite Films

My top 5 movies in no particular order.

Never Let Me Go

Genre:

Sci-fi / Romance

Star Rating:

7/10 Stars

Reasons to Watch:

Stunning cinematography.
Heartwrenching story.
Well-developed charters.

The Devil All the Time

Genre:

Drama / Psychological Thriller

Star Rating:

9/10 Stars

Reasons to Watch:

Highly developed characters with believably intertwined lives.
Engaging throughout.
Compelling, suspensful story.

Penguins of Madagascar

Genre:

Animated Comedy

Star Rating:

8/10 Stars

Reasons to Watch:

Cleveryly written, feel-good story where the "good guys" triumph over the "bad guys" and everyone learns a lesson along the way.
Quite cute and hilarious.

The Thing (1981)

Genre:

Sci-fi / Horror

Star Rating:

9/10 Stars

Reasons to Watch:

Incredibly engaging, suspensful from the first minute until the last.
An ending that leaves you thinking about it for days.

Jurassic Park

Genre:

Action / Sci-fi / Drama

Star Rating:

8/10 Stars

Reasons to Watch:

Well-written character arcs.
Witty, memorable dialogue.
Engaging suspense and action scenes.
All-around one of the best classic summer blockbuster movies to ever be made.
